## 🛡️ Responsible AI & Governance Summary
To maintain absolute compliance with consumer lending protections, the system enforces:
1. **Explainability via SHAP:** Complex ensemble model features are automatically broken down into simple, transparent factors (e.g., translating credit line utilization spikes for customer-facing representatives)[cite: 3].
2. **Mitigating Bias:** Rejects proxy-based variables (like geographic zip codes) that reinforce historical profiling, running active checks to keep impact ratios within fair lending limits[cite: 3].
3. **Human-in-the-Loop (HITL):** Hardship restructuring options, active charge-offs, and critical legal reviews are completely locked out of automation and require standard human approval[cite: 3].
